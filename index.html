What is Crypto Currently?

Crypto Currently was built for the amateur investor who has a limited budget and limited crypto currency knowledge but would like to see what the hype is all about. We at Crypto Currently understand that it can be overwhelming to dive into something when there is an influx of information out there. Therefore, we are making it easier for you to invest based on a personalized questionnaire that will give you current news about coins that fall into your budget and investment criteria. You will be able to read about these coins, watch videos and finally be taken to a purchasing platform to start building your portfolio.
